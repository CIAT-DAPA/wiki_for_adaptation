How to Use

Wiki instructions

The Wiki is designed to help you find, understand, and use indicators, metrics, and standard operating procedures.

Quick Start Guide

1

Search or Browse

Use the search bar on the home page or navigate to the Search Engine to find indicators, metrics, or SOPs

2

Apply Filters

Narrow your search using filters like dimension, type, author, or geographic scale to find exactly what you need

3

Explore Connections

Follow links between related entries to understand the full data ecosystem and find related resources

Understanding the Structure

The wiki organizes information in a hierarchical structure

Indicator

Broad climate system changes or phenomena (e.g., Temperature Anomaly, Sea Level Rise). Each indicator is connected to multiple metrics that measure different aspects.

Metric

Specific quantitative measures used to track indicators (e.g., Global Mean Surface Temperature). Each metric includes detailed measurement methods and is connected to multiple SOPs.

SOP

Standard Operating Procedures - specific data products with technical specifications (e.g., MODIS Land Surface Temperature, GHCN Temperature Records).
