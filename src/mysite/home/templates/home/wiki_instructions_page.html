Understanding the structure

INDICATOR

Attributes of climate change contexts, adaptation practice and adaptation impact that are monitored or evaluated. Each indicator is connected to multiple metrics that measure different aspects.

METRIC

Specific quantitative measures used to track indicators. Each metric includes detailed measurement methods referred to as standard operating procedures (SOPs).
