Climate Adaptation Knowledge Base

Adaptation Tracking Wiki

A comprehensive knowledge base of indicators, metrics and standard operating procedures (SOP) for tracking climate change adaptation in agriculture. The wiki is designed to standardize metrics and their measurement to provide clear, rigorous information on the effectiveness and impact of adaptation efforts. Results should enable policymakers and practitioners to monitor, compare, and accelerate effective climate action.
